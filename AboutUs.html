About Us

Our Story

Education First, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit organization founded by Dr. Blidi Stemn, who has been an educator for over 20 years to help provide quality education to children and youths in rural and underserved communities in Liberia. The organization, formally registered in the United States and Liberia in 2011, has its headquarters in Baldwin New York with additional Offices in Monrovia and Harper, Liberia.

The groundwork for Education First, Inc. began in 2008 when Dr. Blidi Stemn, visited Liberia. As he traveled across the country, he came across overcrowded classrooms with children sitting on lumber as they wrote with their notebooks on their laps. Most of the schools were staffed with under qualified and untrained teachers who taught at grade levels they themselves had barely achieved.

In spite of the problems faced by schools and teachers as a result of the 14 years of civil unrest, Dr. Stemn came across dedicated teachers with limited to no resources teaching in abandoned buildings and open areas. These educators were making the best of the sparse resources available to them at the time. After this trip Dr. Stemn established Education First Academy to provide higher quality education to children and youths in underserved communities particularly in Harper, Liberia. In September 2012, Education First Academy started with 40 children from pre-kindergarten to grade two. The student population has increased tenfold, with approximately 400 children from grades Pre-K through 9 receiving top-tier education. The Academy’s other programs include afterschool tutoring sessions, technology literacy, summer camps, and extracurricular activities.

Our Impact

Education First, Inc. provides educates more than 300 children in Pre-K – 9 through Education First Academy (EFA) in Harper, Liberia which was established in 2012. Additionally, more than 500 public school students in the communities we serve have benefitted from our school supplies program. Approximately 300 teachers and higher education faculty and staff have participated in our professional development seminars and workshops across the country.

Vision and Mission Statement

Our vision is to develop well rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential. We will do this by providing a welcoming, happy, safe, and supportive learning environment in which everyone is equal and all achievements are celebrated.

Our mission is to minimize the future impact of poverty on today’s youths in underserved and rural Liberian communities by providing high quality education in a safe, respectful and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for life-long learning.

Buy A Brick Campaign

Education First, Inc. is soliciting funding to construct its own facility to house Education First Academy in Harper, Liberia. The enrolment at our current facility, which is privately owned, has increased from 40 students in 2012 to 300 in 2016. This $300,000 (United States Dollars) facility when completed will enable us to better provide quality education to approximately 500 students from pre-kindergarten through secondary school. Specifically, the facility will have an early childhood center, a primary school and a secondary school. In addition, the facility will have administrative offices, school health and wellness center, a multimedia center, library, science and technology lab, multipurpose building for indoor activities, and a cafeteria for the school feeding program. The school will also provide community-based programs for adults, particularly women, outside of regular school hours. We believe there are many people like you who wish to contribute to benefit others - so we provide a safe and reliable conduit for people who want to help Liberia’s children from underserved and rural communities aspire to a brighter future, through access to a good education. We guarantee that every penny donated to our not-for-profit organization will go towards the construction of this Education Complex.
