Consulting Services

The Consulting Services at Education First, Inc. encompasses professional development, research, curriculum development, and evaluation in pre-kindergarten through post-secondary education. Some of the projects we are engaged in are job-embedded and out-of-class-professional development for teachers, school administrators, tertiary education faculty and staff In Liberia. We also provide customized workshops for institutions of higher learning, business institutions, and professional and nonprofessional individuals and groups. Services include how to build high performing teams; career success and mentoring; work place employee training; leadership training; customer service; women empowerment; sustaining high-performing employees
