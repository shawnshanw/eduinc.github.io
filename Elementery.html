KINDERGARTEN THROUGH GRADE 6 (K-6)

Education First Academy is proud to partner with our families and community to offer an exemplary educational experience for students. Our curriculum is aligned with the state-adopted Michigan Academic Standards for language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies for elementary students. We are committed to providing all learners with opportunities to acquire the knowledge, skills, and experiences necessary to build meaningful and productive lives in a globally competitive future.

We believe in the power of collaboration to realize such an important mission. Our dedicated teachers use curriculum guides, instructional resources, and technology to build rich learning experiences to support the Forest Hills vision of all learners achieving individual potential.

These curriculum guides are intended to provide a broad overview with highlights of the knowledge and skills students in each grade level are working toward while also providing you with opportunities to enhance learning outside of the school day. This guide is a way to celebrate and build excitement for the learning our students will experience this year!
