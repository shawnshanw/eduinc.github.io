Core Values

AWARENESS

At Education First, Inc., we believe that awareness is one of the foundational pieces of being a global citizen. We believe in the importance of understanding the lives, circumstances, and reality of people and places different from ourselves. We know the value of expanding our knowledge, researching new ideas, and empowering ourselves to connect with people from all over the world. We understand the pivotal role awareness plays in developing empathy. At EFI, we value the role awareness plays in expanding our understanding of diverse perspectives.

BOLDNESS

At Education First, Inc., we believe that boldness plays a pivotal role in innovation. We need to be open to taking risks and trying new, out there, crazy ideas in order to innovate. It takes courage and confidence to do things that haven’t been done before. It takes a certain humility to be willing to try, fail, and then get back up and try again. It takes flexibility and adaptability to thrive in a world that is ever-changing. At EFI, we empower both our staff and our students to live more boldly, speak up, put themselves out there, and to feel safe enough in our community to do so.

CURIOSITY

At Education First, Inc., we believe curiosity is the foundation of learning. We believe it’s monumentally important to live more curiously. We encourage both our staff and students to ask questions, seek answers, investigate the unknown, explore new possibilities, discover new information, and find multiple solutions to real-world issues that matter to them. Curiosity sparks passion. It sparks freedom. At EFI, we know how important it is to never stop being curious.

BELIEFS

At Education First, Inc. we truly believe that every student, whatever their circumstances, holds the innate potential to achieve their goals and succeed in elementary, secondary and post-secondary education and in life. We acknowledge that this belief may often be challenged and tested—either by personal experience in the classroom or external societal pressures—but we are unwavering in our commitment to consistently approach every student not in terms of what they lack, but in terms of what is possible.

RESPONSIBILITY

Achieving excellence requires that we all take responsibility for our role in the success of students, colleagues, and our larger community. Employees maintain a “no excuses” culture in which we push ourselves towards developing effective and sustainable solutions. However, none of us do so alone: We rise together to meet the toughest challenges. Each of us actively plays a part in removing barriers to student success or, when possible, empowering our students to navigate such hurdles themselves.

INNOVATION

At Education First, Inc., we believe innovation is the way of the future. We understand how important it is use creative thinking, channel our imaginations, and come up with new solutions. We know one thing for certain, that our world is ever-changing. We know that our students need to be prepared for jobs they can’t currently envision. Innovation is at the center of being prepared for the future of work. At EFI, we believe that thinking creatively, trying new things, and adapting to changing circumstances are pivotal characteristics of success.

ACCOUNTABILITY

We follow the highest ethical standards and demonstrate honesty and fairness in every action that we take. Measurable outcomes and sound financial management are important elements of our accountability. We expect the same from our partners and those we serve.
